# Code of Conduct

Project Atlas Foundation exists to help people coordinate useful work. That requires a respectful and safe environment.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Explain reasoning clearly.
- Welcome newcomers.
- Give credit for contributions.
- Keep discussion focused on useful outcomes.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, abuse, or threats
- Hate speech or discriminatory conduct
- Spam, scams, or deceptive promotion
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption
- Attempts to capture or privatise community work without transparency

## Moderation

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in removal from the community.

Where safe and appropriate, moderation decisions should be explained publicly.

## Reporting

During the founding phase, report concerns by opening a private communication channel with the repository owner or maintainers. A more formal reporting process should be added once the project has multiple maintainers.
